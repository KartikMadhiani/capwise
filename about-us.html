About Capwise

Capwise is India's first valuation platform built specifically for founders. Our mission is to empower entrepreneurs with transparent, data-driven, and fair startup valuations tailored for the Indian market.

Our Story

Capwise was founded by a team of passionate entrepreneurs who experienced first-hand the challenges of unfair and generic valuation models. We believe every founder deserves clarity, trust, and actionable insights to grow their business and impress investors.

Our Values

  • Transparency: We demystify startup valuation with clear, accessible reports.
  • Trust: Your data is secure and your interests come first.
  • Innovation: Proprietary models and India-specific data for accurate results.
  • Founder-First: Built by founders, for founders.
