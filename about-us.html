About Capwise

Capwise is India's first valuation platform built for founders. Our mission is to empower entrepreneurs with transparent, data-driven, and fair startup valuations.

Our Story

Capwise was founded by a team of passionate entrepreneurs who experienced first-hand the challenges of unfair and generic valuation models. We believe every founder deserves clarity, trust, and actionable insights to grow their business and impress investors.

Trust

Innovation

Founder-First

We are built by founders, for founders. Your success is our mission.
